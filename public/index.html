Homework ACE!

HOMEWORK ACE! was created using PhaserJS.

In this game, you must click on homework as it falls from the sky while avoiding distractions of TV and Videogames.

Game Overview

  • Clicking on Homework adds to your score (50 points!)
  • Clicking on a Distraction disables your ability to click for a short time
  • If homework falls to the bottom of the screen, you miss the points for the assignment
  • If you have missed a homework, there is a chance of an Extra Credit spawning
  • Clicking Extra Credit grants 50 points (no penalty for missing)
  • The game ends when the total score reaches 1000
